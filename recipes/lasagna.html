World's Best Lasagna



Description

If you're looking for a foolproof, crowd-pleasing lasagna recipe, this is it. It's a slow-simmered blend of ground meats, tomatoes, and the perfect balance of herbs and spices, giving it an incredible depth of flavor. The ricotta mixture adds a creamy contrast, and the layers of mozzarella and Parmesan melt beautifully, creating that perfect cheesy stretch with every bite. This lasagna never fails, whether for a family dinner, special occasion, or meal prep. Follow the recipe as written, and you'll have a guaranteed hit every time!

Ingredients

  • Meat of choice (beef & sausage)
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses (parmesean, mozarella and ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let rest.
