Homemade Spaghetti Sauce



Description

This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo is an easy recipe even for beginner cooks, but it's impressive enough for company.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper and garlic salt)
  • Cheese (romano & parmesean)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
